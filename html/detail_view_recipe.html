Instructions

First catch the fish, then kill it. Thereafter, remove the skin and the bones. Bake for 15 minutes. Turn it around and bake for another 30 minutes, Meanwhile prepare the lemon sauce. Get the lemons from the garden. Squeeze them and make a real cool lemon sauce.
